Hello! My name is Lydia, and my online alias is Tabby! I am currently a DMD student on my first year in Raffles Design Institute. I am also an artist. I enjoy digital drawing as a hobby and have been doing so seriously since I was 10.

I mostly draw human /humanoid beings in a stylized manner. My artworks have been described a lot as “anime-ish” or “cartoon-ish”. That said, I am open in learning more artstyles as I go!

Other than drawing, I’m also interested in animating and/or doing motion graph. I have done a lot of personal projects back when I was 10-12, but I’ve unfortunately deleted them so I have no old videos to show.

I use IbispaintX and my phone to draw, and CuteCUT for any motion-graph projects. But I am planning to learn Cavalry and Alight Motion one day.

It had always been a dream of mine to be able to capture a character’s essence faithfully, and to create beautiful and meaningful illustrations. I especially enjoy creating artworks for my friends, watching them light up is something I consider worthwhile. I hope to be able to match everybody’s standards, and to achieve that I’ll need to improve until my skills are worthy being celebrated over.

Check out my artworks in the Gallery section!
